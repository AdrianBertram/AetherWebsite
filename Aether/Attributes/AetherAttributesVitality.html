Your physical toughness is tracked by your vitality. The more vitality points your character has, the more total hit points he has (which is generally useful for all characters). More information on a characters health is can be found under →Hit Points). Vitality also affects your resistance towards fortitude resistance checks (→Fortitude Resistance), which describes your characters resistance towards effects like diseases and poisons (among others). Also, characters with significant amounts of vitality get a natural resistance against damage of any type.

Note, that this ability score applies for all creatures (undeads, constructs etc. are included). For non-living creatures, vitality still represents their sturdiness or effective health.
