Dexterity measures a characters physical agility. It represents a characters reflexes, precision and (physical) balance. Dodging is directly related to this score, as are some weapons as daggers and most notable bows. Especially dextorous characters can dodge deftly are able to use basically any weapon with some effectiveness.

Attack Roll Bonus

This expresses your overall understanding of martial weapons. You get a bonus to all attack rolls made with those type of weapons, whether you're proficient with it or not.

Dodge Bonus

More dexterous characters are more adept at dodging. You gain a bonus on all dodge rolls.

Parry Bonus

More dexterous characters are more adept at parrying attacks with weapons. You gain a bonus on all parry rolls (independent of proficiency).

Precision Combat

I) Precision Combat is a combat skill, which modifies your basic attack. Whenever you make a weapon attack, you can choose to increase the attack time of your weapon by two ticks for this round. If you do, increase the critical thread range of your weapon by one (so for most weapons, you threaten a critical hit on 11-12 instead of just 12). You also get +3 on all critical confirmation rolls made with the weapon. Using this ability costs two stamina.

II) When using Precision Combat, you get a +1 bonus on attack rolls with this weapon this round. Also, the attack time increase of Precision Combat is reduced to one tick (instead of two).

III) When using Precision Combat, a critical thread is automatically confirmed. When you score a critical hit when using Precision Combat, you inflict a grevious wound to the opponent.
