Intelligence

Intelligence determines a characters ability to learn and reason. Wisdom and general intelligence is based completely on this score. Thus, especially wizards and alchemist profit from this score, although rouges can use it aswell. Also, characters with a high Intelligence score are more proficient to focus their spells, thus hitting more precisely (similar as Dexterity works for weapons; →Spell Attack). It also affects your spell complexity ( →Spell Complexity).

When a character reaches a certain intelligence score, he gets a free Skill feat. He still must meet the feat's prerequisites.
